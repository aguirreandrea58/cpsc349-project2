About Us at GetSwole!

Our Goal

GetSwole! was created to help you reach your fitness goal! It has been scientifically proven that setting small goals can help you reach your ultimate goal. Using our planner will help you keep track of your workouts on a day to day basis. We want to attend all age groups, all genders, and all body types.

Who We Are

We are a group of students at CSUF ready to help you start and achieve your fitness journey! As students we know it can get hard and overwhelming setting longterm goals, but we like to take a look at the smaller things that will lead us to the greater picture!
